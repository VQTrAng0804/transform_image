How can I change my image?

You will follow 3 steps below:

- Step 1:

To start, import an image by clicking the "Choose image" button. This will open your file directory, where you can select the desired photo. When you choose your import photo, the "Choose image button" will change to "Selected: your file".




- Step 2:

Choose an effect from the list and click "Transform." Your image will be transformed with the selected effect. If you don't choose an effect and click "Transform," your original image will be displayed.




- Step 3:

Enter a file name for the transformed image. If you leave it blank, it will be saved as "transformed_image.png". Click "Save Transformed Image" to proceed.



*Note:

To select a different effect, click "Main Page" to return to the initial menu.
